Domicile Certificate

Domicile certificate is an essential legal document for proving that a person is having a residence in a State. Domicile certificate allows a person to claim various facilities and schemes provided by the MP government.

Income Certificate

An income certificate is a document issued by an authority under the state government, certifying the annual income of a person from all sources. One of the main reasons to obtain an income certificate is to avail tax relaxations. Income certificate used as a legal document is furnished by individuals who benefit from various subsidies and welfare schemes implemented by the state and central government.

Birth Certificate

Birth or Death registration has been done in the State of M.P. as per Birth & Death Registration Act and M.P. Birth & Death Registration ruling. Accordingly every Birth is to be reported and registered at the place of its occurrence in the prescribed reporting forms.

Application for obtaining Birth Certificate:- This Service of Planning, economic and statistics Department can be avail through various channels enabled by MP Govt. e.g. e-KYC, LSK, CSC, MPO Kiosks. For more details regarding Kiosk location, required document(s), timeline applicable fee etc kindly visit MP e-District Website.

Visit : http://www.mpedistrict.gov.in/

Location : Lok Seva Kendra City : Mandsaur. Pin Code : 458001

Caste Certificate

To provide caste certificate for Scheduled Castes, Scheduled Tribes.

Documents to be enclosed with the application:

  1. 1. For confirmation of caste – Record of immovable property recorded in the name of family member (grandfather / grandmother / great-grandfather / grandfather / father / mother / uncle / brother) (photocopy of land / plot / house registry or any other revenue record etc.) , In which the caste is mentioned. Or a family member (father / uncle / brother / sister / grandfather / father other than blood group) after the year 1996, the caste certificate issued by the Sub-divisional Officer (Revenue).
  2. 2. In the case of the year 1950 or before or after the family MP In order to confirm residence related proofs – Record of immovable property registered under the name of education / government service / voter identity card / family member (grandfather / grandmother / great-grandfather / grand-father / mother / uncle / brother) (land / plot / house Registry or any other revenue record etc.).
  3. 3. Photocopy of academic qualification certificates of the applicant himself.
  4. 4. Enclosed declaration regarding date of caste and residence.

Visit : http://www.mpedistrict.gov.in/

Location : Lok Seva Kendra City : Mandsaur. Pin Code : 458001
